As a world-class broker, your mobile app acts as a 24/7 service hub and a retention engine. The "Quick View" must answer "Am I covered?" and "Is action required?". The "Detailed View" must prevent a support call by answering "What is covered?" and "How do I use it?".

The data structure below utilizes your Policy , Coverage , RiskItem , and PortalAccount  schemas.




1. Investment Life (Unit-Linked / Savings)
Quick View (List Card)

Fund Value: Current Account Value (Integrated feed). Broker logic: Proves wealth value immediately.

Performance Badge: YTD Growth % (e.g., "+5.2%").

Status: Active | Premium Holiday. 

Detailed View (On Click)

Surrender Value: The "cash-out" amount if cancelled today.

Fund Allocation: Split between Equity/Bond/Cash funds.


Beneficiaries: Names of designated recipients. 


Transaction History: Last premium paid date and amount. 


Action: "Top-up Investment" button (via PaymentLink__EXT ).

2. Life & Health (Medical)
Quick View (List Card)


Plan Name: e.g., "Full Health Exclusive". 


Insured Person: Name of the primary insured (useful for family policies). 

Hospital Class: "A-Class / Lux" or "B-Class". Broker logic: Essential for admission.

Detailed View (On Click)


Deductible: The exact amount (e.g., €1,500) per incident. 


Annual Limit: Maximum coverage amount (e.g., €1,000,000). 

Pre-Auth Number: One-tap call button for hospital admission approval.

Excluded Providers: List of non-network hospitals to avoid.


Action: "Digital ID Card" (View Document ).

3. Auto (Motor)
Quick View (List Card)


License Plate: "YZA-1234" (Primary visual identifier). 


Renewal Date: "Expires in 12 days" (Urgency driver). 

Action: "Roadside Assistance" (SOS button).

Detailed View (On Click)


Coverage Tier: TPL vs. Fire/Theft vs. Full Casco. 

Driver List: Named drivers allowed to operate the vehicle.


Green Card: Status (Valid/Invalid) for cross-border travel. 

Accident Care: Phone number for on-site accident recording (Frontida).


Action: "Download Green Card" or "Report Claim" (Trigger FNOL ).

4. Home & Liability
Quick View (List Card)


Property Address: "Akadimias 10, Athens". 


Sum Insured: Total coverage value (Building + Contents). 


Status: Active. 

Detailed View (On Click)


Catastrophe Cover: Distinct "YES" for Fire, Flood, Earthquake. 

Mortgagee: Bank name listed as beneficiary (proof for loans).


Deductible: Retained amount per event (e.g., €500). 

Emergency Services: Direct numbers for plumbers/locksmiths.


Action: "Request Certificate" (Trigger ServiceRequest ).

5. Pet
Quick View (List Card)

Pet Name & Photo: Emotional connection.


Annual Limit Remaining: Progress bar (e.g., €400 used / €1000 limit). 


Renewal: Date of policy expiry. 

Detailed View (On Click)

Microchip Number: Required for vet identification.

Co-Pay: Percentage paid by owner (e.g., 20%).

Waiting Periods: Status of coverage for specific illnesses.


Action: "Upload Vet Invoice" (Trigger Claim / FNOL ).

6. Doctor Civil Liability
Quick View (List Card)


Limit of Indemnity: Max payout per claim (e.g., €500,000). 

Retroactive Date: Date from which past acts are covered.


Status: Active. 

Detailed View (On Click)

Specialty Code: Explicit medical field covered (e.g., "Surgeon").

Jurisdiction: "Greece Only" or "EU Wide".

Extended Reporting: Tail coverage details.


Action: "Download Proof of Insurance" (Trigger ServiceRequest  for hospital credentialing).

7. Marine Motor (Yacht)
Quick View (List Card)

Boat Name / Reg #: Primary identifier.

TPL Status: "Port Police Compliant" badge.


Renewal: "Expires 01/05/2026". 

Detailed View (On Click)

Cruising Limits: Geographic warranty (e.g., "Ionian Sea").


Agreed Value: Hull & Machinery payout amount. 

Tender Details: Reg/Serial of the auxiliary boat.

Action: "Emergency Towing" contact.

Universal Broker Actions (All Policies)
At the bottom of every Detailed View, your app must expose these PortalAccount entitlements:


Pay Now: If PaymentLink__EXT status is open.


File Claim: Triggers Transaction.FirstNoticeOfLoss.


Modify: Triggers ServiceRequest (e.g., Change Address).
