Implementation Note for AgentRise
In Greece, the Agent is the interface.

Bottom of every Detail View: "Call Nikos" (The Agent). Do not route to a generic call center unless it's 3 AM.

Documents: All PDFs (Green Card, Tax Certificate, Hospital Admission Form) must be downloadable via Document URLs stored in your Policy schema.
